Frequently Asked Questions

  • How to seed Aadhaar?
    Visit your bank branch with Aadhaar & account details.
  • How to check DBT enablement?
    Ask your bank to confirm NPCI mapper status.
  • What if my Aadhaar is not linked?
    Submit Aadhaar linking request at the bank immediately.
  • How do I know if I received DBT?
    Check SMS alerts or bank passbook/statement.
  • Is Aadhaar mandatory for scholarships?
    Yes, most scholarships require Aadhaar-based DBT transfer.
