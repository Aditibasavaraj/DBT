Understanding Aadhaar & DBT Accounts

Learn the difference between Aadhaar-linked accounts and DBT-enabled Aadhaar-seeded accounts to ensure you receive government benefits directly and securely.

Aadhaar-linked account

  • Used for identity verification (eKYC) at bank level.
  • Does not guarantee DBT transfers.

DBT-enabled Aadhaar-seeded account

  • Aadhaar seeded to a specific account.
  • Enabled in NPCI/APBS; ready to receive DBT transfers.

Tips & Best Practices

  • Always link Aadhaar to the correct bank account.
  • Verify your DBT status regularly online.
  • Update your bank or Aadhaar details if necessary.
