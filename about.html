About Our Website

ScholarConnect is a digital platform created to spread awareness about Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) and to guide students in verifying their Aadhaar-bank linkage. The website provides simple tools, resources, and interactive modules to help students understand DBT and ensure they can access government benefits and scholarships without hurdles.

Why ScholarConnect?

  • ✔ Easy-to-use DBT checker tool for Aadhaar & bank account seeding
  • ✔ Awareness materials to educate students and families
  • ✔ Scholarship guides and resources
  • ✔ Quizzes and interactive learning to build awareness
  • ✔ Admin dashboard for tracking overall progress

Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between students and government benefit schemes by promoting digital awareness, transparency, and financial inclusion. We aim to empower students so that they never miss out on the support they deserve.
